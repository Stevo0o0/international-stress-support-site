Supportive Resources

Below are resources international students can access:

  1. Campus Counselling Services (Free): Most universities offer free mental health counseling for enrolled students, often with culturally competent providers or international student advisors.
  2. Student-Led Peer Support Groups: Clubs or mentorship programs connecting new international students with experienced peers to share experiences and coping advice.
  3. Local Community Multicultural Centre (Local): Offers workshops, social connection events, and sometimes free mental health navigation for newcomers.
  4. Online Mindfulness Apps (Free tier): Such as Insight Timer or university-provided subscriptions for guided meditations to reduce anxiety.
