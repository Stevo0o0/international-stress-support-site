Supporting International Students as a Society

International students face systemic stressors that individual coping canâ€™t fully resolve. Society and institutions can step in to reduce harm and build resilience.

Three Changes We Can Make

  1. Embed mental health literacy into orientation: Provide mandatory sessions about normalizing stress, available supports, and how to access help early.
  2. Increase culturally competent counseling hours: Offer evening/weekend slots and ensure counselors understand acculturative challenges.
  3. Academic flexibility policies: Implement transparent mental health day allowances and clear extension procedures without stigma.
