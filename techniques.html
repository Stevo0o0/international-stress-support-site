Stress Management Techniques

1. Mindful Breathing

Find a quiet place. Sit comfortably. Inhale slowly for 4 counts, hold for 1, exhale for 4 counts. Focus solely on the breath. If your mind wanders, acknowledge and gently return. Repeat for 5 minutes daily or when overwhelmed.

2. Cognitive Reframing

Write down a stressful thought (e.g., "I'll fail this exam if I make a mistake"). Ask: Is this absolutely true? What’s a more balanced alternative? Replace the original thought with the reframe (e.g., "One question doesn’t define my ability"). Combine with a short walk to reset.

3. Buddy Check-In

Partner with another international student. Schedule a 15-minute weekly check-in. Use prompts: "What was a challenge this week?" "What’s one win?" "Is there something I can support you with?" Sharing normalizes stress and builds accountability.
