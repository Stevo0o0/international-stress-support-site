Tips to Help International Students Manage Stress

  1. Build a peer support network: Regular check-ins with fellow students reduce isolation and normalize struggles.
  2. Create and stick to a routine: Structured sleep, study, and break times help regulate mood and prevent cognitive overload.
  3. Use campus mental health services early: Seeking help before stress escalates is more effective than crisis intervention.
  4. Financial planning: Budgeting and accessing financial workshops reduces anxiety about money.
  5. Cultural identity affirmation: Engage in activities that connect you to your home culture while exploring the new one to reduce acculturative stress.
